About Dev Public School

Established in 2023, Dev Public School has been a beacon of educational excellence for over 2 years.

Our Story

Dev Public School was founded with a vision to provide quality education that nurtures both academic excellence and character development. Over the years, we have grown from a small institution to one of the most respected schools in the region.

Our campus spans over 1 acres and is equipped with state-of-the-art facilities to provide the best learning environment for our students. We believe in creating a nurturing atmosphere where students can explore their potential and develop into well-rounded individuals.

Our commitment to excellence has been recognized through various awards and accolades, making us a preferred choice for parents seeking quality education for their children.

Our Mission

To provide a nurturing environment that fosters academic excellence, character development, and leadership skills, preparing students to become responsible global citizens.

Our Vision

To be a leading educational institution that shapes future leaders through innovative teaching methods, holistic development, and a commitment to excellence.
